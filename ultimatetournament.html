Stage Banning Steps

First Stage Selection: Smash GG will decide who bans first. Bans go P1, P2, P2, P1. Play on last remaining stage. Winner of match bans 2 stages and loser picks a stage. Winner picks character and then Loser picks character. Repeat step 2 until a winner of the set is determined.

Ruleset

Reverse Mains:

Each player tells their opponent who their main is. Each player plays as their opponents character. I.e. if player 1 mains Mario, than player 2 has to play Mario. If a player is a dual main, then that player chooses which of the mains they want to play.

All other rules from Singles apply.
